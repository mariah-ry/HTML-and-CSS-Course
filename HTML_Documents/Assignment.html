Ninjas and Samurai

In feudal Japan, the ninja and samurai were at odds, because the samurai felt that to assassinate their enemies was to kill them without honor. A wikipedia article about ninjas says the following:

"The functions of a ninja included espionage, deception, and surprise attacks. Their covert methods of waging irregular warfare were deemed dishonorable and beneath the honor of the samurai."
