Does Seattle have too many Starbucks locations for its own good?

The answer is yes, it does.

Everywhere you go, there is a Starbucks. Be it any grocery store, Barnes & Noble, or looking across the street.

Maybe it would be better to just get rid of them altogether. But we're not going to do that.

The convenience of Starbucks is something that other coffee shops just don't offer. The mobile
app, the drive throughs, the wide variety of locations. It's good stuff.

I think a better option is just for Starbucks to improve the quality of their coffee. No more lattes that taste like acid at 9:00 a.m.
