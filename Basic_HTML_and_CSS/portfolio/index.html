About

I'm a front-end developer in Seattle, WA hailing from The Tech Academy's Front-End Web Developer bootcamp and am trained in the use of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I got interested in computers after inadvertently becoming a System Admin out of necessity. On that journey, I discovered how much computers can do for people as well as how much of their use is lost on us because of a lack of knowledge and wanted to learn how to help people use them. They can seem complicated, but are only a machines and simply do what you tell them. On that note, I believe the layout and design of a website should be kept simple for three reasons: the developer, the client, and the user.

Good design is as little design as possible. - Deiter Rams
