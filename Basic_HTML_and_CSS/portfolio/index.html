About

I'm a full stack developer in Seattle, WA hailing from The Tech Academy's UI/UX Web Design bootcamp and am trained in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL and more.

I got into the computer field out of necessity in an environment lacking a dedicated System Admin. On that journey of "winging it" and restoring order, I discovered how much computers can do for people as well as how much of their use is lost on us because the subject gets so convoluted. They can seem overwhelming and complicated at times, but a computer is only a machine and simply does what you tell it to.

On that note, I believe the layout and design of a website should be kept simple for three reasons: the developer, the client, and the user.

Good design is as little design as possible. - Deiter Rams
